## Compliance

Sniper complies with the GDPR (the 2018 EU General Data Protection Regulation).

If you need more information about our Privacy and Security Policies, check out the [Privacy Policies](https://sniper.io/privacy-policies).

## Where is my data stored?

Sniper relies on an AWS-managed database located in London (eu-west-2).

## Who can access my data

Only the sniper's creator, his workspace teammates and collaborators he explicitely invited can access the data.

## For how long is my data kept?

We keep the data associated with your account until you delete the information from the account, or the account itself. That means, your information will be kept for as long as you have an account with us and while the Services are provided.

## How can I delete my account?

You can delete your account and all its associated data at any time by sending an email to [support@sniper.io](mailto:support@sniper.io).

## What Cookies does Sniper use?

None! Visiting Sniper's website does not send any information to third-party analytics services. (And of course, Sniper does not use any cookies to display ads, or track you across other sites.)
